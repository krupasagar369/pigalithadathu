Heavy Engineering

Heavy Engineering Casting Excellence

Where innovation meets industrial strength — large-scale, high-integrity castings for construction, mining, and heavy engineering applications.

Get a Quote Learn More

Massive Scale, Uncompromised Precision

Heavy engineering projects demand components of exceptional size, strength, and integrity. Pighalitadhatu's investment casting capabilities extend to large-format castings for construction equipment, mining machinery, port equipment, and power generation systems.

We combine traditional foundry expertise with modern simulation software to optimize casting design, minimize defects, and reduce machining allowances on large components.

  • Large casting weights up to 500 kg
  • High-manganese and alloy steel grades
  • Solidification simulation before production
  • Heavy-duty surface preparation and painting
  • Load testing and proof testing available

Heavy Engineering Products

Large-scale castings for the toughest industrial challenges

Mining Equipment Parts

Crusher jaws, mill liners, bucket teeth, and dragline components cast in high-manganese steel for extreme wear resistance.

Construction Machinery

Excavator buckets, loader arms, counterweights, and undercarriage components for construction and earthmoving equipment.

Port & Marine Equipment

Large diameter gear blanks and ring gears for heavy drives in steel plants, cement mills, and power station auxiliaries.

Press & Forging Dies
