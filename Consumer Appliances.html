Precision Parts for Everyday Living

Consumer appliance manufacturers demand components that are lightweight, durable, and aesthetically pleasing — all at competitive cost. Pighalitadhatu's aluminium and zinc die casting capabilities deliver high-quality parts for washing machines, refrigerators, air conditioners, kitchen appliances, and more.

Our high-pressure die casting process achieves excellent surface finishes ready for anodizing, powder coating, or chrome plating, reducing post-processing time and cost.

  • High-pressure zinc and aluminium die casting
  • Class-A surface finish options
  • Anodizing, plating, and powder coating
  • High-volume production runs
  • RoHS and REACH compliance
