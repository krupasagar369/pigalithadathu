Aerospace & Defense

Aerospace & Defense Casting Solutions

Mission-critical precision cast components for aircraft, defence systems, and space applications — engineered to the highest standards of reliability and performance.

Get a Quote Learn More

Built for Flight and Combat Readiness

Aerospace and defense applications demand absolute precision, strength, and traceability. Our investment casting processes produce complex, near-net-shape components from high-performance alloys including titanium, Inconel, and aluminum alloys — meeting the rigorous requirements of AS9100 and military specifications.

Every component undergoes stringent inspection, including radiographic and fluorescent penetrant testing, to ensure zero defects reach the field.

  • AS9100 Rev D certified processes
  • Titanium, Inconel, and high-temp alloy casting
  • Full first article inspection (FAI) reports
  • MIL-SPEC and NADCAP-approved suppliers
  • Complete material traceability from melt to delivery

Aerospace & Defense Components

Turbine & Compressor Parts

Blades, vanes, impellers, and nozzle components for jet engines and gas turbines in aircraft and power generation.

Airframe Structural Parts

Brackets, fittings, clevis, and structural housings for airframes — lightweight yet able to withstand extreme flight loads.

Avionics Housings

Defense System Parts

Missile & Propulsion Parts

Landing Gear Components

High-strength cast steel and titanium components for landing gear assemblies, torque links, and actuator bodies.
