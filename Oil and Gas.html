Engineered for Extreme Environments

In the oil and gas industry, component failure is not an option. Pighalitadhatu manufactures precision investment cast components that perform reliably in harsh upstream, midstream, and downstream environments — including high-pressure pipelines, subsea applications, and refinery equipment.

We use corrosion-resistant alloys and rigorous testing protocols to ensure every part meets international standards for safety and performance.

  • High-pressure rated valve and pump bodies
  • Corrosion-resistant stainless and duplex steel castings
  • API-compliant manufacturing processes
  • Non-destructive testing (NDT) on all critical parts
  • Custom alloy composition per project requirement

Oil & Gas Components

Built to perform under the most demanding industrial conditions

Valve Bodies & Seats

Gate, globe, ball, and check valve bodies cast from high-grade stainless steel and alloy steel to withstand extreme pressures.

Pump Components

Impellers, volute casings, and pump housings engineered for continuous duty in abrasive and corrosive media.

Pipeline Fittings

Flanges, elbows, tees, and reducers precisely cast for seamless integration into high-pressure pipeline systems.

Pressure Vessels Parts

Nozzles, flanges, and fittings for pressure vessels used in refineries and petrochemical processing plants.

Subsea Components

Corrosion-resistant castings for subsea connectors, manifolds, and wellhead equipment built to last underwater.

Drilling Equipment Parts

Drill collars, stabilizers, and other downhole tool components manufactured for high-stress drilling operations.
