Powering Industrial Machinery

Industrial equipment demands components that can withstand continuous operation, high loads, and extreme temperatures. Pighalitadhatu provides investment cast parts for a wide range of industrial machinery — from heavy-duty gearboxes to precision pump housings and machine tool components.

Our casting solutions reduce machining time, improve dimensional consistency, and deliver better mechanical properties than fabricated alternatives.

  • Heavy-duty alloy and cast iron components
  • Complex internal geometry via investment casting
  • Stress-relief heat treatment available
  • CNC machining and finishing in-house
  • Prototype to full-scale production
