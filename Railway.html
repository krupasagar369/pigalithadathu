Keeping Railways Safe & On Track

The railway industry relies on components that operate continuously under dynamic loads, vibration, and varying climatic conditions. Pighalitadhatu manufactures investment cast parts for locomotives, bogies, couplers, braking systems, and track infrastructure.

Our components are produced in compliance with RDSO and international railway standards, with full material certification and traceability for every batch.

  • RDSO-approved manufacturing practices
  • High-impact and wear-resistant alloys
  • Fatigue and dynamic load tested components
  • Complete NDT: UT, MPT, and LPT
  • Long-life surface coatings and treatments

Railway Components We Produce

Bogie & Wheelset Parts

Bogie frames, axle boxes, and wheelset components cast from high-strength steel for the heaviest rolling stock.

Coupler & Draft Gear

Brake System Components
