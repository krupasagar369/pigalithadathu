PIGHALITADHATU Innovative Solutions Private Limited

"Precision, Power, Prosperity"

PIGHALITADHATU Innovative Solutions Private Limited stands at the forefront of investment casting excellence, delivering precision-engineered components that power industries across the globe. Backed by state-of-the-art manufacturing capabilities and an unwavering commitment to quality, we transform molten metal into high-performance engineering solutions.

Driven by innovation and operational excellence, we cater to diverse sectors with components that meet the most demanding technical and quality standards. Our name, derived from the Sanskrit term for “molten metal,” reflects both our deep-rooted heritage and our progressive vision—blending traditional strength with modern engineering to shape the future of manufacturing.

Core Capabilities

Investment Die Casting

High-precision casting with complex geometry and superior surface finish using steel, stainless steel, aluminum, and superalloys. Tolerance control: 0.1–0.5 mm.

Size: 0.3 kg to 40 kg | Min thickness: 5mm

Shell Casting

Produces high-precision, smooth-surface components by pouring molten metal into a thin shell mold made of sand and resin, ensuring excellent surface finish and dimensional accuracy.

CNC Machining

  • 3-axis to 5-axis
  • High precision tolerances
  • Complex geometry production
  • Automation & efficiency

Reverse Engineering & R&D

In-house R&D team focused on innovation, design optimization, and continuous improvement for high-performance casting solutions.

CAD Designing & Scanning

Advanced CAD tools and 3D scanning technologies to capture, analyze, and recreate complex components with high accuracy.

Prototype Building

End-to-end prototype development enabling quick conversion of CAD designs into functional models with performance validation.

Manufacturing & Quality Control

Robust quality systems including process monitoring, dimensional inspection, and material validation ensuring industry standards.

Agricultural Product Manufacturing

Innovative agricultural solutions designed for durability, efficiency, and performance supporting modern farming needs.
