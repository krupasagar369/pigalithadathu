Powering the Energy Transition

From conventional power plants to solar, wind, and hydro installations, the energy sector needs cast components that deliver decades of reliable service. Pighalitadhatu produces precision investment cast parts for turbines, generators, switchgear, and renewable energy equipment.

Our energy sector castings are designed for maximum durability, thermal resistance, and dimensional stability to minimize maintenance downtime and lifecycle costs.

  • High-temperature alloy castings
  • Power distribution component manufacturing
  • IEC and IEEE standards compliance
  • Solar and wind energy component supply
  • Long-term supply agreements available

Energy Sector Components

Cast components that power industry and communities

Wind Turbine Parts

Hub castings, nacelle frames, pitch and yaw bearing housings for wind turbines across onshore and offshore installations.

Solar Energy Components

Switchgear & Distribution

Turbine & Generator Parts
