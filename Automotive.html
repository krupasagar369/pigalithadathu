Precision Parts for the Automotive Industry

Pighalitadhatu delivers high-quality die cast components that meet the demanding standards of the automotive sector. From lightweight structural parts to complex engine assemblies, our investment casting process ensures dimensional accuracy, surface finish, and material integrity.

We serve leading OEMs and Tier-1 suppliers, producing components that withstand high-temperature, high-pressure environments while maintaining tight tolerances.

  • Aluminum, zinc, and magnesium alloy castings
  • Tight tolerances up to ±0.05mm
  • High-volume production capability
  • Full surface treatment and finishing options
  • IATF 16949 quality standards compliant

Automotive Components We Produce

Precision-engineered parts for every stage of automotive manufacturing

Engine Components

Cylinder heads, engine brackets, intake manifolds, and oil pump housings cast to precise tolerances for reliable performance.

Safety Belt Components

Critical safety hardware including buckle frames, retractor housings, and anchor fittings designed to meet crash safety standards.

Structural Parts

Lightweight structural castings for chassis, body-in-white applications, and suspension components that reduce vehicle weight.

Thermal Management

Heat sinks, cooling brackets, and thermal housings for EV battery management and combustion engine cooling systems.

EV & Hybrid Parts

Motor housings, inverter enclosures, and battery module frames designed for next-generation electric vehicles.

Transmission Parts

Gear housing, clutch components, and transmission brackets with superior dimensional stability.
